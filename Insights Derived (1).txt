# Titanic EDA 

## Objective
Perform Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) and predict survival on the Titanic.

## Contents
- `Titanic_EDA.ipynb`: Full Jupyter Notebook with EDA 
- `train.csv`: Titanic dataset used for training.
- `report.pdf`: Exported notebook as a report.

## Steps Covered
1. Data loading and inspection
2. Handling missing values
3. Visualization of Box plot, Count plot, Violin plot, Histogram, parplot
## Tools Used
Python, Pandas, Seaborn, Matplotlib,
